# Unit Digit Concept

The unit digit of a number is simply the rightmost digit. 

When finding the unit digit of a product, we only need to multiply the unit digits of the numbers.

## Cyclicity

The concept of cyclicity is used to find the unit digit of numbers with powers.
- The unit digits of numbers ending in 0, 1, 5, 6 remain the same for any power.
- The unit digits of numbers ending in 4 and 9 have a cycle of 2.
- The unit digits of numbers ending in 2, 3, 7, 8 have a cycle of 4.
